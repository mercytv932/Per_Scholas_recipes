Chocolate Fudge

Description

Melt chocolate with a little butter and sweetened condensed milk in a pan.

Stir until smooth and pour into a lined tray

Chill in the fridge until firm, then cut into squares and enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Chocolate
  • Butter
  • Sweetened condensed milk

Steps

  1. Melt chocolate, butter, and sweetened condensed milk together until smooth.
  2. Pour the mixture into a lined tray.
  3. Chill in the fridge until firm, then cut into squares.
